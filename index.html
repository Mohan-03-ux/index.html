About BakedWithLove_by Muskan

Our Story

Since 2024, BakedWithLove_by Muskan has been delighting customers in Sambalpur with fresh, handcrafted cakes made from the finest ingredients. What started as a passion for baking has blossomed into a beloved local bakery.

Every cake is made to order with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that each creation is not just delicious, but a work of art. From classic favorites to custom designs, we pour our heart into every cake we make.

"Baking is not just our business, it's our love language. Every cake carries our passion, care, and commitment to making your celebrations unforgettable." - Muskan
